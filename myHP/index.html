IT Pro, Tech Hobbyist, and Cat Lover (with a cat allergy)

Hi, my name is Tarek. I have lived in the San Francisco Bay Area since 2014. My hometown is Brooklyn, NY, but I've also lived in the Midwest for nearly 10 years. After college, I moved to Nebraska and earned my CompTIA A+ Certification, in order to begin an IT career. A couple years later I finished graduate studies to earn a Master of Science degree in Computer Information Systems. My career started by fixing Windows machines and networking in a small jewelry shop. Now, I have been applying web development and other scripting skills along with my troubleshooting and analysis abilities to further my career in support/solutions engineering. My career goal is to become more technically inclined all the time.

Since living in the San francisco Bay Area, my wife and I have been exploring a lot of geography. We enjoy hiking and taking in the beauty of nature, wine cruises on the Bay, and maintain a healthy vegetable garden.

I go to the gym regularly and practice a vegan lifestyle, so I consider myself to be fitness-minded rather than a "health nut." We love animals, especially cats. I don't let the allergies stop me from petting a cat when he/she allows me...
